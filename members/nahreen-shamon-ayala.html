Nahreen is a rising senior at Cristo Rey Atlanta Jesuit High School and a student in the Emory Department of Human Genetics Next Gen Internship program. She is interested in applications of 3D printing for creating affordable assistive devices for individuals with physical disabilities. In the Fong Lab, Nahreen is working on the repair and maintenance of Perkins Braillers as part of the lab’s partnership with the Center for the Visually Impaired. In the future, Nahreen hopes to study biomedical engineering and biomimicry in university.
